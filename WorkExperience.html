Work Experience


Primary School

In fourth year of secondary school I went on a week of work experience in the primary school I attended.

Everyday I had different tasks I had to carry out like correcting homework and photocopying activity sheets.

I spent two days in the lower classes and the last three days in the classroom with 5th and 6th class.

I really enjoyed the week I spent there with the kids and with my past teachers.


Aquarium

While in fourth year in secondary school I spent five days in the aquarium during the halloween break.

These few days of work experience were a lot different than the time I spent in the primary school.

It was different in the way that I felt I was part of the work force. I had daily tasks I had to complete.

My tasks included feeding the fish and cleaning out the tanks.


Kids Club Assistant

Towards the beginning of fifth year in secondary school I started working in a hotel in my local area in a kids club.

I work in the kids club currently. I work during the weekends and during primary school holidays like summer and

christmas break. I take care of the children during their stay in the hotel. My fellow colleagues and I play games

and do activities with the children.
