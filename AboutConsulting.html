Autonomous Things

The term autonomous things is being used more and more in the technology world to talk about

phenomena powered by the latest advancements in artificial intelligence and machine learning.

Autonomous things are things that work autonomously without human guidance or direct intervention

Techopedia (2019)



Autonomous things is one of the most current trends in technology.

The technology is automatically able to make difficult decisions on its own.

Autonomous things take many factors into account in to current situation when making decisions.

An example of an autonomous technology is self driving cars.

Immersive Experience

Thereare two types of immersive experiences, one being when you are actually in a physical environment and the

other where you are shown around a real or imagined environment via desktop, tablet, mobile or via VR headset.

Vismedia (2018)



Immersive experience can be a series of connected 360 degree environments.

Within immersive experience is Virtual Reality, Augmented Reality and Mixed Reality.

By using the 360 technology is allows the user to feel part of the experience.

A huge part of the immersive experience is the use of CGI technology.
